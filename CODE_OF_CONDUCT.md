# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Assume good intent while asking for clarification when needed.
- Avoid harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory language.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Insults, threats, or targeted harassment.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Disruptive comments that repeatedly derail technical discussion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.

Report conduct concerns to `conduct@foxifill.com`.
